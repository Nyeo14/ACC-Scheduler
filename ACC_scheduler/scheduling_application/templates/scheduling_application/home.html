About

The goal of the Sacramento Senior Safety Collaborative (SacSSC) is to provide escort services to seniors in response to anti-Asian hate incidents, such as public outings like shopping, walking, etc. We offer escort services to seniors who feel unsafe or uncomfortable in public without accompaniment. The presence of the escort volunteer will potentially reduce the likelihood of exposure to negative incidents and help participants feel comfortable going about their daily activities.
